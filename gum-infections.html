Treatment of Gum Infections

Gum infections require prompt treatment to prevent tooth loss and other complications. We provide thorough treatment to eliminate infection and restore gum health.

Benefits

  • Stops progression of gum disease
  • Prevents tooth loss
  • Reduces inflammation and bleeding
  • Improves overall oral health

Our Process

  1. 1
    Thorough examination and diagnosis
  2. 2
    Deep cleaning (scaling and root planing)
  3. 3
    Antibiotic treatment if needed
  4. 4
    Follow-up care and monitoring

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about treatment of gum infections

Common signs include bleeding gums, swelling, bad breath, and loose teeth.
